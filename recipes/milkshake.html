Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A classic and delightful beverage that combines the rich and creamy goodness of vanilla ice cream with the smooth texture of milk. The primary ingredients include vanilla ice cream, milk, and sugar, which are blended together until they form a thick and luscious drink. The vanilla flavor is typically derived from vanilla extract or vanilla bean, giving the milkshake a sweet and aromatic taste. Some recipes may also include whipped cream on top for an added indulgence, and a cherry or sprinkles for garnish. The result is a refreshing and satisfying treat that is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

    Step 1:

  • Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth;

    • Step 2:

  • pour into glasses and serve.
